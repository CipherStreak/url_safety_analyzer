Understanding Safety Ratings

üî¥ Harmful (1-2 Stars)

Red glowing stars indicate known malicious sites, phishing attempts, or severe security risks. Do not visit these sites. They may steal your data, install malware, or compromise your device.

üü° Neutral (3 Stars)

Yellow stars indicate unverified or suspicious sites with limited reputation data. Exercise caution. Avoid entering personal information or downloading files from these sites.

üü¢ Safe (4-5 Stars)

Green stars indicate trusted, verified sites with valid security certificates and no known threats. These sites are generally safe to visit, but always remain vigilant about what information you share online.

Security Best Practices

  • Always verify the URL before clicking, especially in emails or messages
  • Look for HTTPS and a padlock icon in your browser's address bar
  • Never enter sensitive information on sites rated as harmful or neutral
  • Keep your browser and security software up to date
  • Use strong, unique passwords for different websites
  • Report suspicious URLs to your IT department or security team
